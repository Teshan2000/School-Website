About Mahinda Rajapaksha College


Mahinda Rajapaksha College, located in Homagama in Colombo District in the Western Province Sri Lanka, is one of the country's leading schools. Mahinda Rajapaksha College, which is the largest secondary national school in South Asia provides learning facilities over 6850 students. Since the journey started in 2011, Mahinda Rajapaksha College has been dedicated to prepare students with unique diversities and talents to success their life. School's rich culture often tend to achieve areas of excellence of the school range from various academic successes to various sports and athletic victories.

Headmaster's Message

It is with great pride and immense pleasure that I extend a warm welcome to all of you to Mahinda Rajapaksha College, a place where academic excellence meets character development and lifelong learning. As the Headmaster of this esteemed institution, I am deeply committed to fostering an environment that nurtures every student's potential, allowing them to grow into well-rounded individuals who are not only academically competent but also compassionate and responsible members of society. We believe our school upholds the rich cultural heritage and values of Sri Lanka while embracing modern educational practices. Our dedicated team of educators strives to create an inclusive and supportive learning atmosphere, where students are encouraged to explore their interests, think critically, and challenge themselves to reach new heights. We are fully aware of the trust you place in us as your partners in your child's education. Hence, we take our responsibility seriously and work tirelessly to provide a safe and stimulating environment that nurtures creativity, curiosity, and a passion for lifelong learning. Our commitment to academic excellence is reflected in our rigorous curriculum, supplemented by a wide range of co-curricular and extracurricular activities that cater to diverse talents and interests. From sports to arts, music to debate, we strive to provide every student with a platform to excel and showcase their unique abilities. As we embrace the challenges and opportunities of the future, we remain steadfast in our mission to prepare our students to become compassionate global citizens who contribute positively to the world.
