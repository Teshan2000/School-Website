About Mahinda Rajapaksha College

Mahinda Rajapaksha College, located in the Homagama of Colombo District in Uva Province Sri Lanka, is one of the country's leading schools. Mahinda Rajapaksha College, which is the biggest secondary national school in south asia provides learning facilities over 6850 students. Since its opening in 2011, Mahinda Rajapaksha College has been dedicated to preparing students with unique diversities and talents. Areas of excellence range from various academic successes to various sports and athletic victories.
