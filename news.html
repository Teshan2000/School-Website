News

Welcome New Headmaster

Mr. G.D. Sarath Bandu Gunasekara was appointed as the new principal of Mahinda Rajapaksha College after the honourable departure of Mr. Kasun Gunarathne in 2023.

A-Level Results 2022

Results of G.C.E. Advanced Level Examination 2022 has released yesterday, and our school had received many achivements this year. All the students who participated A/Ls has passed in number 763.

Herman Loose Achievement

Our 'Group A' and 'Group B' Cadet platoons have achieved the All Island Herman Loose 11th place and the 16th place from 42 troops respectively. Specificity is that all the cadets were under 16.

12 Years of Excellence

Our school is celebrating the 12th Anniversary this year. Traditionally, there will be a chanting programme and a dana programme for adorable monks from 19th January night to 20th morning.

10th Anniversary Tribute Song

School students has made a wonderful song as a tribute to our school for celebrating 10th Anniversary this year. The song will be released on 20th January, 2021 when is the school's 10th Birthday.

A-Level Results 2021

Results of G.C.E. Advanced Level Examination of 2021 had released yesterday, and approximately 300 students of our school has passed this year. Also, we received Colombo district 2nd and 3rd places.

O-Level Results 2021

G.C.E. O-Level Examination Results of 2021 has brought a glory to our school, as for the first time all the 763 students passed O/L exam. Therefore, we achieved a 100% excellent result with 95 '9A' passes.

Debate Competition on Swarnawahini

On 26th of August Ranisu Koongalage student will participate to the "Nadiya" live debate competition at 10 p.m. on channel Swarnawahini. We hope you all may vote for him in that day.

Tae-Kwon-Do All Island 3rd Place


Island 3rd Place and Western province 1st place of Tae-Kwon-Do Tournament has achieved by the Vensara Kalhara student from our Mahinda Rajapaksha College Homagama.

A-Level Results 2020

G.C.E. A-Level Examination Results of the year 2020 has released yesterday, and our school had performed 99.6% excellent results with 45 '9A' passes and 97 '8A' passes this year.

A-Level Results 2019

Yesterday, G.C.E. Advanced Level Examination Results of 2019 has released and 72% of A/L students of our school had passed. This year, our school has island 2nd place from the Faculty of Technology.

O-Level Results 2016

G.C.E. Ordinary Level Exam Results of 2016 has released, and the first batch of our school had brought many achivements this year. These students are the pioneers of our school and passed 668 students from 673.
